The Exchange.

A GAMARAY wheel is not a sunk cost. It is an inheritance that travels with the car.

Every set of GAMARAY wheels carries something beyond its material and its design. It carries a standing relationship with the atelier — one that does not end when the car changes hands. The Exchange Program formalizes that relationship into something transferable, documentable, and permanent. For the original owner, it is a reason to return. For the next owner, it is a privilege they inherited the moment they acquired the car.

Conditions

How it works in practice.

  • The inheritance is determined by the original classification recorded in the GAMARAY Registry — Signature or Bespoke
  • The privilege transfers automatically to every registered subsequent owner alongside the structural warranty
  • The inheritance applies exclusively toward a new fully bespoke GAMARAY commission
  • One inheritance per set traded in — once applied to a commission it is fulfilled
  • Wheels must be returned to the GAMARAY atelier in their original condition
  • Condition assessment conducted at no charge prior to confirming the Exchange
