Bespoke Wheels · GAMARAY

BESPOKE WHEELS

Designed With You.
Built for No One Else.

A fully bespoke wheel begins with the car, its proportions, and the person behind it. Together, we define the geometry, fitment, finish, and personal details. The final set is engineered and forged in aerospace-grade 6061-T6 aluminum exclusively for your commission.

  • Original geometry developed from first principles
  • Finish and materials selected with you
  • Optional personal engraving
  • Spare wheel included
  • Lifetime structural warranty
  • Five-year road protection
  • GAMARAY Registry and NFC Certificate of Assurance

BESPOKE EXHAUST

The Voice of the Machine.

Sound is a design material. Every GAMARAY exhaust is engineered around the engine, the vehicle, and the tonal character requested by its owner.

What We Engineer
  • Material & Character: Titanium or stainless steel, selected for tone as much as performance
  • Optimized Flow: Scavenging, back pressure, and valve configuration calibrated to your engine
  • Engraved Identity: A name, a date, initials, carried quietly on every system
  • All systems carry a Provenance Warranty: structural coverage that transfers with the vehicle
Materials

T304 Stainless Steel — Polished silver or ceramic coated in bronze, black, or white.

Titanium — Polished silver or heat-finished in gold, blue, or purple.

GAMARAY Signature Configuration

Every system is protected by the transferable GAMARAY Provenance Warranty.

Porsche commissions are configured with ceramic coating and an active valve system by default. Both may be adjusted to suit the commission.

Other Vehicles

Ferrari. Lamborghini. McLaren. Rolls-Royce. Aston Martin. If it deserves the finest exhaust, we will build it. Every commission begins with a conversation.
