The Art Commission

The Wheel as Canvas.

Art collectible embedded in another collectible.

A living program where artists worldwide transform performance into art, and art into motion. Artists from any discipline are invited to interpret a GAMARAY wheel as their medium. The resulting work becomes part of the GAMARAY Collection: documented, exhibited, and in select cases, produced as a limited physical edition carried on the vehicle itself.

Grand Commission · Annual
Grand Commission

One artist. Full production. Physical work installed on a client vehicle. Premiered at the GAMARAY Atelier Night, an intimate gathering of collectors, press, and invited artists.

Emerging Voice · Annual
Emerging Voice

For artists under 30. Digital-first work. Full Archive inclusion and production grant. The credential of being numbered in the GAMARAY Collection early carries its own weight.

Sensory Prize · Annual
Sensory Prize

Best use of the wheel's unique properties as a medium — a surface that lives in two states: still sculpture and kinetic art. Any medium, any career stage.

Open call · Rolling · No deadline

Artists submit a concept brief and portfolio link. No fee. No PDF. The GAMARAY jury reviews quarterly. Artists may also be nominated by existing program participants. The open call is always live; there is no gate on when you can begin.
