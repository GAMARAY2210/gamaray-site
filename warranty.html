Warranty.

Three coverages. One permanent record.

A GAMARAY wheel is not a part. It is a commission, drawn, forged, and finished by the hands that built it. This warranty is not a legal minimum. It is a permanent record of craftsmanship, and a promise that travels with the object for as long as it exists.

When the Vehicle Changes Hands

The Transfer Registration.

Structural coverage transfers to every subsequent owner, but transfer is not silent. The new owner must contact GAMARAY within 30 days of acquiring the vehicle. Until registration is confirmed, coverage is suspended. Once registered, it resumes in full, permanently.

Exhaust Systems · Provenance Warranty

Exhaust Assurance.

Stainless Steel Systems: Lifetime

Structural integrity, weld strength, and material consistency. Designed for both road and track. Normal cosmetic evolution, improper installation, or external damage are not covered.

Titanium Systems: 3 Years

Manufacturing defects and material inconsistencies. Surface coloration is a natural characteristic of titanium under heat, it is not a defect. It is evidence of the material performing exactly as intended.

Catalytic Converters: 3 Years

Manufacturing defects and structural failure under normal conditions. Leaded fuels, non-approved additives, or aggressive tuning calibrations void eligibility.
